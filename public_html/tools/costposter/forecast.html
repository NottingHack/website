Forecasting

The charts below forecast the finances for the two years after May 2013 of Nottingham Hackspace. They are based on our figures to date, and are obviously not guaranteed!

Income and Outgoings

This chart assumes that our membership grows at the rate we've experienced over the last 12 months, and our outgoings are pretty fixed (with rises for inflation and an average misc cost taken into account.)

As you can see, by the end of this year we are taking in more than £1,000 a month above our costs, and over £2,500 above by April 2015.
