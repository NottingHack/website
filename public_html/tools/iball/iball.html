iBall / Spacewriter DSN lookup

Hello you have likely found you way to this page from the Nottingham Hackspace Google post Anyone got an iBall / spacewriter LED sphere with software?
This page is a way to look-up the Device Serial Number (DSN) needed by the SDE software.
